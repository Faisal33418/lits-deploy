# 💕 LITS - Standalone Platform Positioning

## What LITS Is

**LITS (Love In The Sky)** is an **independent, standalone dating and social networking platform** exclusively for airline employees.

### Core Identity

- 🎯 **Single Purpose:** Dating and connections for airline staff
- 🔒 **Standalone:** Not part of any other app or service
- ✈️ **Aviation-Only:** 100% focused on airline industry
- 🌍 **Independent:** Self-contained platform with own ecosystem

---

## What LITS Is NOT

❌ **Not a travel booking app**
❌ **Not a staff travel tool**
❌ **Not integrated with airline systems**
❌ **Not part of Super AI StaffTraveler**
❌ **Not affiliated with any airline**
❌ **Not a general dating app with airline features**
