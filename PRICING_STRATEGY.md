# 💰 LITS Pricing Strategy

## Overview
Freemium model with aviation-focused premium features, inspired by Tinder's tiered approach but optimized for airline professionals.

---

## 🆓 Free Tier - "Ground Level"

**What's Included:**
- ✅ Create profile with aviation details
- ✅ Company email verification
- ✅ 10 swipes per day
- ✅ See mutual matches
- ✅ Basic chat with matches
- ✅ View profiles in your base city
- ✅ See compatibility scores

**Limitations:**
- ❌ Limited swipes (10/day)
- ❌ Can't see who liked you
- ❌ No schedule-based matching
- ❌ No layover discovery
- ❌ Ads displayed

## 🏆 Competitive Analysis

### Tinder Pricing (for comparison)
- Tinder Plus: $9.99-14.99/month
- Tinder Gold: $14.99-29.99/month
- Tinder Platinum: $19.99-39.99/month

### LITS New Pricing (More Accessible)
- Cruising Altitude: **$10.99/month** (competitive with Tinder Plus)
- First Class: **$15.99/month** (lower than Tinder Gold)
- Captain's Choice: **$22.99/month** (more accessible than Tinder Platinum)

### Why Lower Pricing Makes Sense:
1. **More inclusive** - Accessible to ramp agents, mechanics, provision agents (lower income brackets)
2. **Higher conversion rates** - Lower barrier to entry = more paid users
3. **Market penetration** - Easier to gain market share from Tinder/Bumble
4. **Volume over margin** - More subscribers at lower price = better total revenue
5. **Aviation-specific value** - Unique features still justify premium over free
6. **Better retention** - Affordable pricing = longer subscriptions
7. **Word-of-mouth growth** - "Only $11/month!" is easier to recommend

## 🎨 Psychological Pricing

### Why $14.99 instead of $15?
- Appears significantly cheaper
- Industry standard

### Why 3 tiers?
- **Decoy effect** - Middle tier looks best value
- Most users choose middle option
- Premium tier for status seekers

### Why annual discounts?
- **Upfront revenue** for growth
- **Higher retention** (sunk cost)
- **Predictable income** for planning
